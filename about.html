Pei-Yu Cheng

Associate Professor

Dr. Pei-Yu Cheng is an associate professor in the Department of Information Management at Tamkang University, Taiwan.

He received his Ph.D. in the Department of Engineering Science from National Cheng Kung University in 2020.

His main research interests include Education technology, artificial intelligence applications in education, and Learning Analytics.
